What is the best place to live in Europa at 2023?

The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU) has ranked 173 cities around the world according to a number of factors, including healthcare, education, infrastructure and the environment. Once again this year, Vienna comes out on top. The Austrian capital unreservedly ticks all the boxes, once again emerging as the best city in the world in which to live. Its health and education systems were praised by the magazine, and its investment in culture and entertainment was widely praised by the Economist.

Where is the most expensive place to live?

For the wealthy as well as everyone else, the cost of living has climbed over the past year as prices rose. Some cities were hit harder than others. The Julius Baer Lifestyle Index, which ranks the world's most expensive cities annually, finds that Asia is the costliest region for luxury living, with Singapore ranking as the world's most expensive city.
